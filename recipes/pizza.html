Pizza

Description

A simple pepperoni pizza that the whole family will enjoy.

Ingredients

  • 1.25 cups warm water
  • 1 tsp yeast
  • 3 cups bread flour
  • 1.5 tsp salt
  • 0.25 cup olive oil
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can pizza sauce
  • 8 oz mozzarella cheese
  • 0.25 cup pecorino romano cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F.
  2. Combine water and yeast in bowl. Let sit for 5 minutes.
  3. Combine flour and salt in stand mixer bowl. Pour in yeast mixture. Knead dough for about 7 minutes.
  4. Grease large bowl with olive oil. Form dough into tight ball. Place in bowl and let rise until doubled in size.
  5. Mix tomatoes and pizza sauce in a small bowl.
  6. Grease rimmed baking sheet with olive oil. Roll dough onto baking sheet. Add sauce, cheese, and toppings.
  7. Bake pizza on bottom rack for 17-20 minutes.
